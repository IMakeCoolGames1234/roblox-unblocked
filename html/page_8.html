Parental controls

Roblox offers a variety of parental controls to help you support your child’s experience on the platform. These enable you to control the type of content your child has access to, how much money they can spend, and how much time they are allowed to spend on Roblox each day.

For teens, you can see valuable insights and important information about their activity on the platform. You can view who your teen has connected with, see a breakdown of how much time they spend on Roblox and in which specific experiences, and opt in to receive spending notifications.

First things first

You will need a Roblox account linked to your child's account to manage parental controls on behalf of your child.

Create account

When creating an account with parent privileges, you'll be asked to enter your birthday, verify your age using a government-issued ID or credit card, and link to your child's Roblox account.

By linking your account to your child's, you can view and update parental controls from your own device. You also get access to insights about your child's Roblox usage, such as their daily screen time and on-platform connections.
